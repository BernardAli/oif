Our story

Built by young leaders for young leaders.

OIF began with a conviction that the next generation does not only need inspiration. They need access, mentorship, structure, accountability, and communities that call out their best.

That conviction now lives across public programs, mentorship cohorts, outreach, partner relationships, donor stewardship, and a secure member dashboard that keeps the work organized.

Vision

Teaching, mentoring, and training youth in leadership and entrepreneurship.

Mission

Creating platforms where accomplished personalities meet, mentor, and guide emerging leaders.

What holds us

Values with operational weight.

These are not decorative words. They shape how programs are designed, how leaders serve, and how the foundation handles trust.

i.

Excellence

Careful preparation, elegant delivery, and thoughtful follow-through.

ii.

Community

Formation happens through relationship, shared courage, and consistent support.

iii.

Integrity

Transparent stewardship, role-based accountability, and respect for people.

iv.

Impact

Success is measured in lives strengthened and communities served.
